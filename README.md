# 591-resource-management
## Intro
This game is told in two parts, the first trakes place at the start of our timeline and involves a tower defense game.  
The second part is a cross between plague inc and cookie clicker involving the takeover of a galxy
## Controls
### General
* Select which part you would like to play from the main menu
### Part 1
Click on units and then a grid space to assign the tower to that space, go back to the lab to upgrade and see win progress
### Part 2
Click on passive upgrades on the right side of the screen and tap the sun to spend/earn currency
Switch to the upgrade tab to purchase more potent scaling upgrades


## Narrative [SPOILERS]
### Part 1
Part 1 of the game is the first event in our timeline, taking place long before the events of game 1.  Where a rogue sect of humanity long departed form the main cluster attempts an invasion on an alien society.  This society in a last desperate bid develops a bioweapon that may ultimately doom the universe.  After the events of this game the society will have successfully repelled the invaders who will all die on planet, unfortunately the disease slowly begins to spread to the weapon's creators as such they make the decision to destroy all space faring technology on the planet and lock everyone in a final metallic tomb in hopes that it may save the universe before it is too late, turning their planet into a barren tomb with a festering core.  Which will lay undisturbed until the events of Cosmogen.

### Part 2
Takes place after our third game, involves spreading through the solar system and neighboring galaxies in a cookie clicker style game, the game ends when you have become unstoppable and spread through multiple galaxies in the local cluster.  The universe is doomed, there are remaining questions to consider about who's fault this was and whether this could have been avoided but ultimately in the fate of the universe retrospective questions like that mean little.
